About Us

Our mision and our vision

Our mission is to facilitate global access to the immense wealth of literary knowledge stored in the Gutenberg Library and other similar repositories. We are committed to providing an exceptionally fast and accurate book search platform, powered by cutting-edge technology. Our passion is to connect people to the wisdom of written words and enrich their lives through the discovery of classic and contemporary literature.

Our vision is to lead the online book search industry, being recognised as the standard of excellence in accessibility and search speed. We seek to pioneer technological innovation, developing solutions that consistently exceed our users' expectations. As we evolve, we aspire to become a global benchmark in the democratisation of knowledge, giving people around the world the ability to explore, learn and enjoy humanity's vast literary heritage.

Our History

Our company was founded in 2023 at the University of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria as part of a mandatory Big Data group project. During that project, a group of passionate students came together to tackle complex data challenges and discovered a passion for technology and data analytics. As the project progressed, they decided to take their experience and expertise into the business world, and LiBook was born.

Our Evolution

Our company began as a modest programming assignment in college, when a group of enthusiastic data science and engineering students decided to tackle the challenge of creating a search engine for books in the Gutenberg domain. Initially, they developed a simple Python engine that could perform basic searches in the vast repository of books. However, their passion for technology and problem solving soon led them on a transformative journey.

The team immersed itself in the world of Java application development, creating essential components such as the 'crawler' to download books, the 'cleaner' to clean the data, the 'indexer' to index it and the 'query' to handle queries efficiently. As the applications gained complexity, so did the infrastructure. The company embraced the containerisation trend and adopted Docker to efficiently package and deploy its applications. Nginx technology became a key enabler to optimise performance and query speed. The result of these improvements was astounding.
